The Man Behind the Vault
ABKC International Senior Judge

John Enerva

Born in Pangasinan, Philippines. Now based in Alberta, Canada. John Enerva has dedicated over 20 years of his life to the American Bully breed — as a breeder, exhibitor, community leader, and judge.

He was the first person living in Asia to become an official ABKC judge in 2014. In 2019, he judged the ABKC Nationals in Houston — the biggest American Bully show in the world. He has judged across the USA, Italy, Guam, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam and of course the Philippines.

The American Bully Vault is his knowledge — 20+ years of firsthand experience, direct conversations with community leaders like Arlon Berberabe, Dave Wilson, Ed Shepard, Marco Suarez, Ron Ramos, Rolando Mata, James Cooper and many others and deep study of the breed — made accessible to everyone.
